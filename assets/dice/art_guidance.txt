# Dice Asset Art Guidance

## Overview
Six-sided dice faces for encounter resolution system.
Rendered on Canvas (not DOM elements).

## Dice System Design

**Color Coding**:
- **Red** = Strength/Combat (sword icon)
- **Green** = Agility/Escape (running figure icon)
- **Blue** = Magic/Special (star/magic symbol icon)
- **Blank** = No effect (plain white/gray, no icon, no color)

**Critical Rule**: Colored faces ALWAYS have icons. Blank faces NEVER have icons or colors.

## Dice Mechanics Reference

**Each player boat has**:
- 2 Red dice (Strength)
- 2 Green dice (Agility)
- 2 Blue dice (Intelligence/Magic)

**Each die has**:
- 3 colored faces (with icon)
- 3 blank faces (no icon, no color)

**Usage**:
- Roll dice on encounters (combat, escape, events)
- Count total colored faces showing
- Red for combat damage
- Green for escape/agility checks
- Blue for magic/special effects

**Rendering**:
- Dice rendered on Canvas during encounters
- May show rolling animation
- Display final results clearly
- Visual feedback for which faces contribute

---

## Generation Tips
1. **Flat design preferred** - Easier to render/animate on Canvas
2. **High contrast** - Icons must be clearly visible
3. **Simple icons** - Recognizable at small sizes (dice may be 32-48px on screen)
4. **Consistent style** - All four faces should match aesthetically
5. **Transparent background** - For Canvas compositing
6. **Clear color coding** - Red/Green/Blue immediately distinguishable
7. **Blank faces are BLANK** - No icons, no bright colors

## Technical Notes
- Rendered on Canvas using `ctx.drawImage()`
- May be rotated/animated during dice roll
- Size scaled based on UI layout
- Click/tap to roll dice
- Results calculated from random face selection
- Visual representation of game mechanics (not just decorative)
